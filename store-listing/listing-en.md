**GastroCore POS — The Complete Restaurant Management Platform for Swiss Gastronomy**

Run your restaurant smarter. GastroCore POS is the all-in-one point-of-sale and restaurant management solution built specifically for the Swiss market — from independent bistros to multi-location restaurant groups.

---

**🇨🇭 Built for Switzerland**
GastroCore POS is designed from the ground up for Swiss gastronomy. Full MWST (Mehrwertsteuer) compliance with automatic tax calculation for dining-in (8.1%) and takeaway (2.6%) orders. Compliant with Swiss nDSG data protection regulations and GDPR. Works in German, French, Italian, and English.

---

**📴 Offline-First — Never Lose a Sale**
Network outages won't stop your business. GastroCore's offline-first architecture keeps your POS fully operational without internet. Take orders, process payments, and manage tables — all locally. Data syncs automatically when connectivity is restored. No cloud dependency for day-to-day operations.

---

**🛒 Omnichannel Ordering**
Meet your guests wherever they are:
- **Table Service** — Intuitive table layout with real-time order status
- **Kiosk** — Self-service ordering kiosk mode for fast-casual concepts
- **Online Ordering** — Branded web ordering for pickup and delivery
- **QR Order & Pay** — Guests scan, order, and pay from their phones
- All channels feed into one unified kitchen display and reporting system

---

**📱 The 6-App Ecosystem**
GastroCore is not just a POS — it's a complete restaurant operating system:
1. **POS** — Full-featured point of sale for waitstaff and cashiers
2. **Kiosk** — Self-service ordering terminal
3. **Kitchen Display (KDS)** — Real-time order management for the kitchen
4. **Online Ordering** — Customer-facing web app for takeaway and delivery
5. **Manager Dashboard** — Business analytics, reports, and remote management
6. **Admin Console** — Multi-location configuration and staff management

All six apps share one data model, one menu, and one reporting layer.
