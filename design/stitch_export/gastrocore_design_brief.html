# GastroCore POS - UI/UX Design Brief ## Project Overview Restaurant POS system for Android tablets (10" primary). Dark theme, touch-optimized, offline-first. Target: Swiss and German restaurants - modern, clean, fast. ## Design System Specifications ### Colors - **bg-primary:** #0f1117 (Main background) - **bg-secondary:** #1a1d27 (Panels, sidebars) - **bg-card:** #222633 (Cards, buttons) - **accent (blue):** #4f8cff (Primary actions) - **green:** #34c759 (Success) - **orange:** #ff9500 (Warning/Kitchen) - **red:** #ff3b30 (Error/Urgent) - **text-primary:** #f0f0f5 - **text-secondary:** #8e8e9a ### Typography - **Font:** Inter / SF Pro / Roboto - **Scale:** 10px to 32px+ - **Weights:** 400 to 800 ### Spacing & Radius - **Base:** 4px - **Radius:** 8px, 12px, 16px ## Core Screens (Phase 1) - **S01: PIN Login:** Staff login with user avatars and PIN pad. - **S02: Shift Opening:** Cash entry to start the workday. - **S03: Main POS:** 3-column layout (Categories, Products, Order Panel). - **S04: Modifier Selection:** Modal for product options. - **S05: Payment:** Split payment and method selection. - **S06: Receipt Preview:** Thermal paper simulation. - **S07: Shift Close:** Stats and cash counting. ## Restaurant Mode (Phase 2) - **S08: Floor Plan:** Visual table management. - **S10: KDS:** Kitchen display for order tracking. ... and more.
