2 Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the GastroCore POS service. The categories of data we process are:

2.1 Business & Operator Data

  • Restaurant name, address, and contact information
  • VAT/UID number for MWST compliance
  • Subscription and billing information
  • Staff accounts: name, email, role, PIN hash

2.2 Transaction Data

  • Order details: items, quantities, prices, modifiers
  • Payment records: amount, method, timestamp (no full card numbers stored)
  • Table assignments and session data
  • Receipt copies for statutory retention

2.3 Customer Data (Online Ordering)

  • Name and email address (for order confirmation)
  • Delivery address (if delivery is enabled)
  • Phone number (optional, for order updates)
  • Order history (retained per customer request or statutory requirement)

2.4 Device & Technical Data

  • Device identifier and OS version (for app diagnostics)
  • App version and crash logs (anonymised)
  • IP address (for cloud sync connections, not stored long-term)
We do not sell your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3 Offline Storage

GastroCore POS is designed with an offline-first architecture. All operational data — orders, menu, staff sessions — is stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database (SQLite with SQLCipher).

  • Local data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • No internet connection is required for core POS operations
  • Local data is never shared with third parties without your explicit action (e.g., cloud sync)
  • Data remains on your device until you explicitly delete it or uninstall the app

The operator (restaurant owner/manager) is responsible for securing access to the physical device on which GastroCore POS is installed.

4 Cloud Synchronisation

Cloud sync is optional. When enabled, your data is synchronised to our secure cloud infrastructure hosted in Switzerland (ISO 27001 certified data centres).

What is synced (when cloud sync is enabled)

  • Transaction records and sales data
  • Menu configuration
  • Staff accounts and permissions
  • Device configuration

Data transfer safeguards

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Data at rest in the cloud is encrypted using AES-256
  • Data is processed within Switzerland or the EU/EEA
  • Any transfers outside Switzerland comply with Swiss nDSG Chapter 5 requirements

You can disable cloud sync at any time in the app settings. Existing cloud data can be deleted upon request (see Section 8 — Your Rights).

6 Analytics

We collect limited, anonymised analytics to improve the app's stability and performance:

  • Crash reporting via Firebase Crashlytics — anonymised stack traces, no personal data
  • Performance metrics — app load times, sync durations (aggregated, not per-user)
  • Feature usage — which screens are used (aggregated counters only)

We do not use behavioural advertising or cross-app tracking. Analytics data cannot be used to identify individual end customers of the restaurant.

You can opt out of analytics in the app settings under Settings → Privacy → Analytics.

8 Your Rights

Under GDPR (for EU/EEA residents) and the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nDSG), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — Obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to statutory retention obligations
  • Right to restriction — Restrict processing while a dispute is resolved
  • Right to data portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@gastrocore.ch. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) in Switzerland, or your local supervisory authority within the EU/EEA.

9 Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest (local device and cloud)
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • Role-based access controls and least-privilege principles
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Incident response plan with 72-hour breach notification (per GDPR Art. 33)
  • Staff trained in data protection annually

No system is completely secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@gastrocore.ch.

10 Children's Privacy

GastroCore POS is a business application intended for use by restaurant operators and staff. It is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@gastrocore.ch.
