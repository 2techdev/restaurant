2 Description of Service

GastroCore POS is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) restaurant management platform comprising:

  • POS App — Point-of-sale application for Android devices
  • Kiosk App — Self-service ordering kiosk application
  • Kitchen Display System (KDS) — Kitchen order management display
