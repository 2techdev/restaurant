## App Title
GastroCore – Restaurant POS

## Short Description (max 80 characters)
Offline-first restaurant POS. Swiss MWST compliant. KDS. TWINT & card payments.

## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

**GastroCore** is the complete point-of-sale system built specifically for Swiss restaurants, cafés, and food service businesses. Designed to work even without internet, GastroCore keeps your restaurant running smoothly no matter what.

---

### 🍽 Complete Restaurant POS

Take orders at the table or counter with a fast, intuitive interface built for hospitality professionals. Browse your menu by category, add items with a tap, apply modifiers, and send orders to the kitchen instantly.

- Fast item search and category browsing
- Modifiers and special instructions per item
- Multi-course ordering with course grouping
- Split bills and partial payments

---

### 🇨🇭 Swiss MWST Compliant

GastroCore handles Swiss VAT (MWST) automatically across all three restaurant rates:

- **8.1%** — food and beverages served on-site
- **3.8%** — accommodation and hotel breakfast
- **2.6%** — take-away and non-alcoholic drinks

Every receipt clearly shows VAT per rate with the correct legal breakdown. Z-reports and shift summaries include full VAT accounting ready for your fiduciary.

---

### 👨‍🍳 Kitchen Display System (KDS)

Replace paper tickets with a real-time kitchen display. Orders appear on the KDS the moment they are placed, with colour-coded timers showing urgency. Kitchen staff mark items done, and the POS updates automatically.

- Real-time order display with elapsed timers
- Colour-coded urgency (green → orange → red)
- Per-item done-checking
- Separate KDS views per station (grill, cold kitchen, bar)

---

### 💳 Multiple Payment Methods

Accept any payment your guests prefer:

- **Cash** — with automatic change calculation
- **Card** — via Wallee or MyPOS terminals
- **TWINT** — Switzerland's leading mobile payment
- **Split payment** — divide the bill any way you need

---

### 🪑 Table Management

Manage your dining room with a drag-and-drop floor plan. See at a glance which tables are free, occupied, or reserved. Assign staff to tables, track how long guests have been seated, and move orders between tables effortlessly.

---

### 📡 Offline-First Architecture

GastroCore works fully offline. Your local database stores all orders, menus, and transactions. When connectivity returns, everything syncs automatically. No internet = no problem.

---

### 📊 Reports & Z-Reports

Close your shift with a complete Z-report including:

- Total revenue by payment method
- VAT breakdown by rate
- Top-selling items
- Per-waiter performance
- Shift duration and transaction count

---

### 🌍 Multi-Language

GastroCore speaks your team's language:
**German · French · Italian · English**

Switch language per device — perfect for multilingual teams.

Get started for free. The free tier includes full POS functionality for small venues. Upgrade when you need advanced reporting, multi-terminal sync, or KDS.
