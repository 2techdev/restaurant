Features

Everything Your Restaurant Needs

A complete point-of-sale solution built specifically for the hospitality industry.

🍽️

Table Management

Visual floor plan editor with drag-and-drop table layouts. Manage multiple rooms and track table status in real time.

📋

Order Management

Fast order entry with product categories, modifiers, and notes. Split bills, merge tables, and handle group orders with ease.

💳

Payment Processing

Accept card payments via Wallee and MyPOS terminal integrations. Cash handling with change calculation built in.

📦

Product Catalog

Manage your full menu with categories, prices, descriptions, and availability. Quick search and barcode scanning support.

👥

Staff Management

Multiple employee roles with PIN-based login. Track orders per waiter and manage access permissions by role.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Daily sales summaries, product performance reports, and shift closing procedures. Export data for your accounting software.

🔌

Offline-First

All core features work without an internet connection. Never lose a sale due to connectivity issues. Data syncs when back online.

🖨️

Receipt Printing

Bluetooth and network printer support. Customisable receipt templates with your restaurant's branding and logo.

☁️

Optional Cloud Sync

Back up your data and access it across devices with optional cloud synchronisation. You stay in full control of your data.

2

3

Start Serving

Take orders, process payments, and run your restaurant — all from one intuitive interface.
